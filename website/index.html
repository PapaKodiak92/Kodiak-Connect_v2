Free Family Accounts

Family accounts built for safety.

Family Accounts are planned to help parents stay involved without making communication confusing. A Head of Family account will be able to create or invite child accounts, approve access to new spaces, review safety alerts, and manage key privacy controls.

  • Create and invite child accounts
  • Approve friend requests and server joins
  • Receive risky chat and safety alerts
  • Manage privacy, contacts, and safer defaults

Paid Business Accounts

Secure workspaces for teams.

Business Accounts are planned as paid workspaces for companies, teams, and organizations that need more control. Businesses will be able to create secure servers, organize departments, manage access, and host meetings or amphitheatre-style sessions inside Kodiak Connect.

  • Business-owned secure servers
  • Private channels and role permissions
  • Meetings and amphitheatre-style calls
  • Guest access, audit tools, and offboarding
