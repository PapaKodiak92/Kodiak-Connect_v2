Business Workspace Terms

Effective Date: June 4, 2026

Business Accounts and paid Business Workspaces are planned future features and are not currently live.

Contact: support@kodiak-connect.com

1. Current Status

Kodiak Connect does not currently collect payments and does not currently offer paid Business Workspaces as a live production service.

These terms describe the planned direction and will need to be updated before paid business features launch.

2. Planned Business Workspaces

Kodiak Connect may later offer business-owned workspaces with:

  • Business servers or workspaces.
  • Private channels.
  • Role-based permissions.
  • Admin controls.
  • Guest access.
  • Meetings or amphitheatre-style sessions.
  • Offboarding and workspace management tools.

3. Workspace Ownership

For future Business Workspaces, the organization or person that creates or pays for the workspace may control workspace settings, membership, permissions, and business content, subject to applicable law and Kodiak Connect policies.

4. Administrators

Business workspace admins may be able to manage users, channels, roles, settings, and content within their workspace. Admins are responsible for using those tools lawfully and responsibly.

5. Users in Business Workspaces

Users who join a Business Workspace should understand that workspace owners and admins may control workspace access, settings, and data inside that workspace.

6. Payments

No payments are currently collected. If paid services are added, pricing, billing, refunds, cancellation, taxes, and subscription terms will be provided before payment is accepted.

7. Compliance

Business users are responsible for determining whether Kodiak Connect is appropriate for their legal, regulatory, industry, employment, recordkeeping, or confidentiality obligations.

8. Updates

These terms will be updated before paid Business Workspaces launch.
