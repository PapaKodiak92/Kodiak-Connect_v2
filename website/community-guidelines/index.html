Community Guidelines

Effective Date: June 4, 2026

Kodiak Connect is being built for clean, practical, secure communication. These guidelines explain the behavior expected in shared spaces.

1. Be Respectful

Do not harass, threaten, bully, stalk, or abuse others. Disagreements are allowed. Targeted abuse is not.

2. Keep Spaces Safe

Do not share exploitative, illegal, malicious, or dangerous content. Do not encourage self-harm, violence, or harm toward others.

3. Protect Minors

Do not sexualize, exploit, groom, manipulate, or endanger minors. Report child safety concerns to support@kodiak-connect.com.

4. Do Not Spam or Scam

Do not send spam, phishing links, scams, fake giveaways, impersonation attempts, or malicious files.

5. Respect Privacy

Do not share private information about other people without permission. Do not share private messages, addresses, phone numbers, passwords, tokens, or account details to harm or expose someone.

6. Use Admin Tools Responsibly

Admins, moderators, future family managers, and future business workspace owners must use control tools responsibly and only for legitimate safety, organization, or workspace needs.

7. Report Problems

Report abuse, safety issues, account problems, or policy concerns to support@kodiak-connect.com.
