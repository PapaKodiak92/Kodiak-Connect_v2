Copyright and DMCA Policy

Effective Date: June 4, 2026

Kodiak Connect respects intellectual property rights.

Contact: support@kodiak-connect.com

1. Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe content on Kodiak Connect infringes your copyright, send a notice to support@kodiak-connect.com with:

  • Your name and contact information.
  • A description of the copyrighted work.
  • A description or location of the allegedly infringing content.
  • A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, agent, or law.
  • A statement that the information in your notice is accurate.
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act for the owner.
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

2. Counter-Notices

If your content was removed because of a copyright complaint and you believe the removal was mistaken, you may contact support@kodiak-connect.com with a counter-notice.

3. Repeat Infringers

Kodiak Connect may suspend or terminate accounts that repeatedly infringe copyrights.

4. Abuse of Copyright Process

False or abusive copyright claims may result in account action or legal consequences.
